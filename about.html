Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Grown with Care

From a small balcony garden to a full botanical studio — this is the story of Leaf & Bloom.

The Beginning

Born from a Garden

In 2018, our founder Johnnel Artiaga transformed his passion for rare botanicals into a curated studio tucked in the heart of Cebu City. What began as hand-delivering lovingly potted plants to neighbours quickly blossomed into something extraordinary.

Today, Leaf & Bloom is a full-service botanical studio serving clients across the Philippines — from intimate home collections to grand corporate biophilic installations and once-in-a-lifetime weddings.

Every plant we carry has a story. Every arrangement we create is a piece of living art. We believe that surrounding yourself with nature is one of the greatest luxuries in the world.

Johnnel

Founder & Lead Botanist
